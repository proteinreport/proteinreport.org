The free press release distribution platform for companies and organizations addressing protein industry challenges and developing novel solutions.

To meet our eligibility criteria, press releases must:

  • Describe scientific, industry, commercial, or policy information first released within the past 90 days
  • Offer non-promotional news value - releases that are primarily promotional may not be accepted
  • Be written in a native level of English

Protein Report may accept or deny submissions at its sole discretion. See the Newswire terms for more details.

Have questions? Send an email to hello@proteinreport.org to learn more.
